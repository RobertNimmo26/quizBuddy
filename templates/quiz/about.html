Quiz, quiz, quiz!

QuizBuddy provides a platform for both students and teachers to make studying more interactive! Teachers can create classes and make quizzes for their students. Want to make a change from optional revision quizzes and grade them instead? We got your back! You have forgetful students? Then all you have to do is use our built-in tool to send them reminders!

Buddy for Life

Students of all ages face demotivation at a certain point throughout their studies - hence to aid them fight this off we created quizBuddy! Students are equipped with a buddy at the start - with an EGGtastic buddy, to be exact! However, if they feel more of a connection with the magical girl cat or the mischiveous unearthly kid then they have the choice of continuing their journey with them!
